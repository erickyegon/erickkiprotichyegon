About Me

I am a globally recognized epidemiologist and data scientist with over 15 years of experience leading transformative health initiatives across Africa. As the Global Director of Performance, Evidence, and Insights at Living Goods, I integrate advanced analytics, AI, and machine learning to improve community health outcomes, particularly in maternal and sexual and reproductive health rights (SRHR).

My career spans leadership roles at organizations like the International Center for Research on Women (ICRW) and Ipas, where I’ve managed $25M+ projects and driven policy changes through evidence-based advocacy. I’m passionate about mentoring the next generation of public health professionals, supervising PhD students, and teaching biostatistics.

My mission is to leverage data to tell stories that change lives, ensuring equitable health access for underserved communities.
