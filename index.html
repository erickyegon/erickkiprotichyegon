About Me

I am a globally recognized epidemiologist, data scientist, and public health leader with over 15 years of experience driving evidence-based change across Africa. I bring together advanced analytics, machine learning, and AI to improve maternal and sexual and reproductive health rights (SRHR), especially for women and girls in underserved communities.

In addition to my technical expertise, I’m deeply committed to knowledge sharing and mentorship. I serve as academic supervisor for several PhD students in public health and nursing, and I have previously taught Biostatistics at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology.
